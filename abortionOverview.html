Abortion Services Overview

Abortion appointments are typically available within one week.

At Boulder Valley Health Center, we stand firmly in support of abortion rights and the right to choose, including for patients under 18.

Call us at 303-442-5160 to schedule an abortion appointment. Abortion appointments cannot be booked using our online scheduling tool.

Patient-Centered Abortion Care

Our clinic offers safe, respectful, and patient-centered abortion services for people of many ages, genders, and backgrounds.

  • Confidential care: We prioritize your privacy and provide a non-judgmental environment.
  • Understand your options: Learn about medication and procedural abortion options and what to expect.
  • Know your rights: We can help you understand the legal protections for abortion access, including for minors.
  • Financial support: Our team can help answer questions about costs, insurance, and financial assistance, including travel support.
  • Dedicated support team: You are not alone; our staff will guide you through each step.
